Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Workday to Google Calendar (“the App”) helps you convert a course schedule file (XLSX) into Google Calendar events. The App does not operate a server or database and does not sell or share personal information.

What data we access

  • Google Calendar data necessary to create calendars and insert events that you explicitly request.
  • The content of the XLSX file you select locally in your browser.

How data is used

All processing occurs in your browser. Calendar events are created in your Google account via Google’s official APIs, using the permissions you grant during sign-in. We do not persist, transmit, or store your XLSX contents, calendar data, or tokens on our servers (we don’t operate any).

Data retention

We do not retain personal data. If you remove the created events from your Google Calendar, they are deleted by Google.

Third parties

Google Calendar APIs are used only to create calendars and insert events. We comply with Google API Services User Data Policy.
